Documentary filmmaker, videographer and photographer with over 10 years of professional experience. Specializing in events, construction documentation, documentary production, weddings, corporate video, and music & arts coverage across Canada and internationally.

Recent work includes a short documentary filmed in Pond Inlet (Mittimatalik), Nunavut, for the Safe Savvy Kids Foundation — presented at the Arctic Snappers Expo in Iqaluit, April 2026. Bachelor's degree in Filmmaking, DUOC UC, Chile. Fluent in English and Spanish.
